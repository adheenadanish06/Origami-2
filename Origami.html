Chameleon

Interesting facts about Chameleon

  • These species come in a range of colors, and many species have the ability to change color.
  • Unlike many lizards, chameleon's can't grow their tails.
  • Calumma hilleniusi is known to actively cross streams while hunting. There are photos of them swimming in Necas' book as well as a pic of one feeding while partially submerged in the water.

Pigeon

Interesting facts about Pigeons

Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
Both female and male pigeons share responsibility of caring for and raising young.
Pigeons are highly sociable animals.
